intro

I am a Ph.D. student at the USC Robotic Embedded Systems Lab in Los Angeles, CA. My goal is to make autonomous quadcopters fly quickly and gracefully through unknown environments. I also helped design and build USC's Crazyswarm 49-quadcopter research platform.

Before joining RESL, I worked as a professional software developer on industrial 3d scanning tools at Geomagic and the JMP visual statistics package at SAS. I completed a B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Photography at The Evergreen State College in Olympia, WA.
